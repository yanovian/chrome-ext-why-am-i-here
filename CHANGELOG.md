# Changelog

Updates for people who use Why Am I Here? Newest first.

## 1.3.0

- **Getting-started tutorial** — step-by-step guide for setting a goal, browsing, and responding to nudges.
- **Privacy policy** published — confirms everything stays on your device.
- Now available on the **Chrome Web Store**.

## 1.2.0

- **Larger toolbar icon** — easier to spot at a glance.

## 1.1.0

- First public release.
- Set a browsing goal in the popup; optional, never forced.
- **Two kinds of nudges** — distraction reminders when you drift off-topic, and rabbit-hole check-ins when you go too deep on the right topic.
- Tracks **active time** and **tab alignment** against your goal (local only, no accounts or analytics).
- Gentle **!** badge and popup prompts — no system notifications.
- **Configurable thresholds** for both reminder types in Settings.
